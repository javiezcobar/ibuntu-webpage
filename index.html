iBuntu Lix Sur

As close as possible to macOS

iBuntu Lix Sur marks the beginning of a whole new era in our development. For the first time we (developers) use KDE instead of Gnome as Desktop Environment. This gives the user tons of options for customization and modification as well as an astonishing look & feel which makes you forget you are not using a “real” Mac here.
